Instructions..

1) Player one moves first. He enters a cell number, between 1-25.
2) He finds a number - x, hidden in that cell.
3) Now, the second player moves. He enters a cell number, again between 1-25.
4) If the number revealed is same as the number revealed by player 1, then player 2 scores. If, he gets a different number, no one scores. If he opens the cell opened by the first player, again, no one scores.
5) Now, player 1 moves again.
6) And the game continues.... (until all the numbers are revealed)
